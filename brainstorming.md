# Build Team The Musical
## For for any ideas, suggestions, comments, questions, queries, quibbles, quams, or quarrels.


**Proposed structure:**

Exposition  
Inciting incident -> Major dramatic question  
Complications  
Crisis -> Answer major dramatic question  
Denouement  

What the protagonist wants and why they can’t have it  
&ensp; &ensp; To be happy with their place in Build Team.  
&ensp; &ensp; &ensp; &ensp; They think build team is boring, they think they can do everything alone (character flaws)  
&ensp; &ensp; To leave the Tech Dump world and return to the real world  
&ensp; &ensp; &ensp; &ensp; They don’t have a way out yet. They need to build something that will let them escape.  

How the protagonist grows:  
They learn that everyone has unique skills, and they need to work together.  
They learn that what happens in Build Team is not actually boring.  

Act 1:

[Exposition]  
Set up the world, introduce characters  
Minor conflicts in the real world  
Protagonist in unsatisfied with role in Build Team, dreams of something more exciting  

[Inciting incident]  
They have an argument with the other members  
&ensp; &ensp; *Can they collaborate with the rest of Build Team?*  
Transported through the Tech Dump  
Explore world  
Meet antagonist  
Goal is to go back to the real world  
&ensp; &ensp; *Can they make it back?*  

Act 2:

[Complications]  
Increasingly difficult conflicts as protagonist pursues goal  
Continue to explore world, meet allies  
Successes and setbacks develop their character  
They begin to see the value of working together  
Brought to a point of hopelessness, protagonist thinks they’ll never get back  

Act 3:

[Crisis]  
Climax featuring face off between protagonist and antagonist  
Uses what they learned along the journey to defeat antagonist  
Return to real world  

[Denoument]  
Become functional member of Build Team
