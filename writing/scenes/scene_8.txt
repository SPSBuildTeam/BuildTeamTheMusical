Scene 8: TECH DUMP FOREST - NIGHT 2X:00 PM

Immediately following scene 7. The Scientist and Assassin look at each other and shrug, then walk to stage right. There are random pieces of junk strewn about, and they begin collecting them. John and Money Guy stroll to stage left.

JOHN BERNOULLI
Look Money Guy, I don’t know who you are, but thank you for talking me out of that back there. I’m sorry, but I have to ask a favor of you.

MONEY GUY
(Nervously) Anything… anything at all.

JOHN BERNOULLI
We’re going to need a distraction, and you know Ssinrok best out of all of us. Can you and the Scientist go up and try to talk with him, maybe pretend that you want to join his side again? We just need to buy some more time.

MONEY GUY
Umm… sure I can. Certainly I can.

JOHN BERNOULLI
Perfect.

All meet at center stage.

JOHN BERNOULLI
Alright team, here’s how this is gonna go. We know Ssinrok is probably still looking for us, so you, Doctor, and you, Money Guy, will go and distract him. Pretend you want to join him. Meanwhile, the Assassin and I will work on a way to defeat him. Sounds good to everybody?

SCIENTIST
Sorry, what was that part about me joining that scoundrel who destroyed my village?

JOHN BERNOULLI
Yes, that’s exactly right. Let’s do this.

John and the Assassin walk stage left and start tinkering with the parts. The Scientist and the Money Guy walk stage right, Ssinrok enters stage right.

SSINROK
There you are. But where is that John Bernoulli?

MONEY GUY
We don’t know. He and the Assassin left us. Behind. Ssinrok, we want to join you.

SSINROK
Ha! You really think it’s that easy? I don’t want traitors like you on my team. But, if you tell me where John is, I may just spare your lives.

SCIENTIST
We told you, we don’t know where he is. I’ve personally had quite enough of Mr. Bernoulli. He thinks he’s better than everyone. He may act like he’s changing, but when it comes down to it, he doesn’t trust anyone but himself.

SSINROK
If you two can’t help, then get out of my way. I must find him.

There is suddenly a commotion from John and the Assassin.

JOHN BERNOULLI
No, it should go like this! Here, give me that.

ASSASSIN
Quiet, John. Look, if you think you know everything, just do it yourself, alright?

SSINROK
	(Looks past the Scientist and Money Guy)
Aha! There he is.

Ssinrok fires the air cannon (or some kind of projectile). John jumps away, exposing the Assassin. The blow strikes her squarely in the chest, and she falls backwards almost offstage, and lays unmoving.

JOHN BERNOULLI
No!

SSINROK
Alright, all three of you, line up there.

John, The Scientist, and Money Guy line up center stage.

JOHN BERNOULLI
I’m so sorry everyone. This is all my fault.

SSINROK
Quiet! It’s time to show you what it’s gonna be like when I’m in charge around here.

The Assassin comes up from behind and whacks Ssinrok on the head with the stick. He slowly falls to the ground. Everyone is quiet for a few seconds.
