Scene 3: MAGICAL FOREST - X:00 PM

P is looking around frantically, as if hearing noises coming from all directions and trying to see into the dark undergrowth.

JOHN BERNOULLI
This sure is a scary forest, why is there no one else here?

A rustling noise is heard from the bushes and John Bernoulli whips his head in the direction of the sound.

JOHN BERNOULLI
(Scared) Is is is somebody there?

More rustling but no response

JOHN BERNOULLI
(Scared) H-hello?

False Korniss jumps out of the bush quickly and energetically

FALSE KORNISS
Greetings young man!

JOHN BERNOULLI
Gee Willickers! You scared me! Who the hell are you?

FALSE KORNISS
I am Dr. Korniss, esteemed physicist and keeper of beasts large and small.

JOHN BERNOULLI
I’m John Bernoulli, I am lost in these woods and trying to get home. What do you mean by beasts?

FALSE KORNISS
(Gesturing to the scenery around him)
There are horrible beasts out there in the world. They are harbingers of pestilence and war, famine and disease. I created them to protect physicists like myself while we worked. One day, an engineer stumbled upon me and my beasts in the forest. He saw their potential and he convinced the beasts to go with him.

JOHN BERNOULLI
Why did you create these beasts if they bring so much suffering?

FALSE KORNISS
(Sigh) They were corrupted by the engineer. He sought to use the beasts as the backbone of his new inventions. When the beasts realized they were going to be used like machines, they became angered at the engineer and all those around them who used his inventions. They destroyed the engineer’s village, and all who lived there. Now they roam the countryside, looking for more villages and engineers to eliminate from this world.

JOHN BERNOULLI
That’s horrible! I can’t believe people would try to use the beasts like that.

FALSE KORNISS
Only beings as corrupted as man could corrupt creatures as pure as the beasts. But the beasts are simple-minded. Now that they have been angered, they will not stop until they are killed. You must go out and slay all of the beasts before they destroy the villages of the world in anger.

The low growling rumble of a beast can be heard coming from nearby. John Bernoulli jumps at the sound.

JOHN BERNOULLI
(Scared) What was that?

FALSE KORNISS
Do not be afraid, that is the one beast who stayed with me. He was not corrupted by the engineer. I want you to take him with you on your quest. He will stand loyally by your side.

JOHN BERNOULLI
I don’t know about this. I don’t think I’m cut out to go on a hunting spree. And quite frankly, I just met you. Don’t you think it's a tall ask for me to risk my life to clean up your mess? I’m only in this forest because I want to go back home, not to get involved in some stranger’s mess.

FALSE KORNISS
Well we have introduced ourselves, so now we are friends, not strangers. And I see potential in you John. You’re not the first person I’ve encountered here, but you’re the first I’ve asked to help me.

JOHN BERNOULLI
I’m sorry Dr. Korniss, but I’m not special and I don’t have the time or the ability to slay the beasts if I’m ever going to get home.

FALSE KORNISS
I can lead you home, but only if you slay the beasts. It is too dangerous for me to lead you while they are still out there. Slay them, and return to me, and I will guide you home.
(Sticks out hand for a handshake)
Deal?

John Bernoulli thinks it over for a second.

JOHN BERNOULLI
(Shaking False Korniss’s hand)
Deal!

FALSE KORNISS
Excellent! (Pointing stage right) Follow this path to the east until you reach the nearest village. It shouldn’t take more than half a day. I will command my beast to follow you, but he is shy and he will probably not show himself until you emerge from the forest. Good luck John.

JOHN BERNOULLI
Thank you Dr. Korniss. One more question, what is the beast's name?

False Korniss
Stephen Van Rensselaer, he will respond to his full name only.

John Bernoulli nods and starts to follow the path towards stage right.
