Scene 9: TECH DUMP FOREST - NIGHT 2X:00 PM

Scene 9 picks up exactly where scene 8 left off. One might even ask why they are not the same scene. 9 is a much nicer number than 8 though. John Bernoulli stands with Assassin, money guy, and scientist.

SCIENTIST
Ssinrok is gone for good!

MONEY GUY
He can’t menace the innocent people of our world any longer.

JOHN BERNOULLI
It’s finally over.

ASSASSIN
Wait… what’s that?…… It’s a key!
(Picks up the key)

SCIENTIST
Maybe it unlocks that strange tiny wooden door in the corner.

JOHN BERNOULLI
Let’s try it.

NARRATOR
They unlock the door with the key that they very conveniently found. However, it doesn’t lead to anywhere they recognize. It leads to a small room with tables, shelves, and oddly, a giant motor. It is crammed with strange bottles, wires, wood pieces, tools, and probably anything else you can think of.

(SCENE CHANGE)

MONEY GUY
What is that room?

NARRATOR
Keen listeners may recognize it as-

JOHN BERNOULLI
The Build Team room! Oh thank goodness!

ASSASSIN
The Build Team room?

JOHN BERNOULLI
That’s where I came here from! I can go home!

SCIENTIST
(Looks at the open door curiously) Is it some sort of laboratory?

JOHN BERNOULLI
Uhhh. Not exactly?

MONEY GUY
You’re leaving?

JOHN BERNOULLI
Yeah, I have to get back home. 

SCIENTIST
Well, it’s been a pleasure. Do lots of cool physics in that lab of yours! 

JOHN BERNOULLI
It’s not a… oh, whatever. Thanks!

MONEY GUY
We couldn’t have defeated Ssinrok without you. And because of you, I’m going to follow my dreams and be a physics teacher.

ASSASSIN
You definitely know how to have a good adventure. And… I’ll miss you. Stay safe.

JOHN BERNOULLI
Aww, I’ll miss you too. Don’t die again any time soon. I wouldn’t have survived without your help. Without all of your help. And you’ve all taught me so much.

SCIENTIST
All in a day’s work as a scientist!

MONEY GUY
And physics teacher.

Everyone looks at the Assassin.

ASSASSIN
Yeah, no. Not my thing. (Fondly) Just go home already.

JOHN BERNOULLI
Alright, alright, I’m going. Bye!!

NARRATOR
John Bernoulli steps through the door into the Build Team room, and the portal closes behind him.

J does a dramatic step. Assassin, money guy, and scientist get offstage somehow, and members of Build Team are now onstage.

Narrator: He looks back to see…

(SCENE CHANGE)

JOHN BERNOULLI
The portal was in the fridge!? Huh, I always wondered what was in there.

BUILD TEAM CAPTAIN 
John!? Where were you, it’s 7:56?

OTHER MEMBERS
Yeah!/ Where were you?/ What were you doing?/ etc.

JOHN BERNOULLI
I was… uh… at the tech dump. But where I was doesn’t matter, I have some things I wanted to tell you all.

Everyone is surprised.

BUILD TEAM CAPTAIN
What? You do?

JOHN BERNOULLI
I’ve realized a few things in the past… uh… hour. I’m sorry for everything I said earlier. Build Team is awesome, and everything we do- teaching middle schoolers included- is really important. You all are my friends, and I want you to know that. 

MEMBERS
Thanks!/ Build Team is awesome!/ etc.

JOHN BERNOULLI
(Starts singing the Build Team theme song)
Sing it with me!

Everyone sings the Build Team theme song.
