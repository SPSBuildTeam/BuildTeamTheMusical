Scene 6: TECH DUMP FOREST - MIDDAY

John, the Assassin, and the Scientist are walking through a clearing. All three are weary from the long trek after leaving the village. John looks up from studying his map, confused.

JOHN BERNOULLI
I don’t understand. The map says the last beast should be right here.

ASSASSIN
John, we’ve been walking around these woods forever. Can’t we just rest?

SCIENTIST
You must be misreading it. Let me have another look.

Suddenly, they all freeze. The Assassin turns around so that the three are standing back to back in the middle of the clearing. An eerie silence covers the scene. A loud crash stage right. All three look that direction, False Korniss enters stage left. John looks back first.

JOHN BERNOULLI
(Relieved) Oh, Korniss, it’s you! We haven’t been able to find the last beast anywhere.

SSINROK
Well John, I suppose I should tell you the truth. But first I must congratulate you.

JOHN BERNOULLI
On what?

FALSE KORNISS
On defeating my two challenges, of course.

ASSASSIN
What do you mean your challenges?

SSINROK
I’ve been watching you, John. You’ve proved quite the capable young physicist. Personally, I didn’t have that high of hopes for you, especially after your first encounter with my monster.

ASSASSIN
I don’t like where this is going.

JOHN BERNOULLI
Look Korniss, I don’t know what you’re talking about. The only thing I wanna do is defeat this third beast and get outta here. Could you just show us where it is?

SSINROK
There is no third beast, John. The monster, the pie tin babies, they were all there to test you. And my real name isn’t Korniss. I am Ssinrok.

SCIENTIST
So that was your doing? Those pie tins ravaged my village for weeks, and all for what, this silly little game you’re playing?

FALSE KORNISS
But it’s not just a game. John has proved his worthiness, by his victory against my monster and his ingenious strategy for getting rid of those pie tins. John, I’m here to offer you a position in the new ruling class.

ASSASSIN
We helped with those things too! And besides, he doesn’t want to join you, Korniss.

John Bernoulli stares, enchanted, at False Korniss.

SSINROK
Doesn’t he? Let’s hear what he has to say.


JOHN BERNOULLI
What kind of position?

SSINROK
I’m assembling the best and brightest to take over this world. We’ll run things the way they should be done, on engineering principles.

A pause, John still enraptured and slowly drifting toward False Korniss.

SSINROK
John, I took a chance on you. I’m not sure why exactly. Maybe I saw a bit of myself in you. Prodigious, self assured… but still unpolished. You can do great things if you join me.

ASSASSIN
Don’t do it John, it’s a trap!

SCIENTIST
It’s not worth it! Think of us, think of the townspeople. What kind of leader would do this stuff?

SSINROK
Don’t let these people decide for you. This is your dream, the chance to be free from others tying you down. Leave those little physics demos behind, join me in engineering, that’s where the real work gets done.

Another sound from stage left. Money Guys enters. Everyone looks over, John momentarily breaks out of the trance.

MONEY GUY
Wait!

SSINROK
Leonard, I wasn’t expecting you yet. Take these two away.
(Gestures towards the Assassin and Scientist)

MONEY GUY
Ssinrok, I’m- I’m done following your orders. John, you need to listen to me. A long time ago, Ssinrok told me the same things he’s saying to you now. At the time I knew him only as Korniss, the well-meaning physicist who sought my help to clear the land of the beasts.

SSINROK
That’s enough, Leonard.

MONEY GUY
I passed his tests, just like you. He gave me the promise of a better world, he told me I would never have want of money, or fame, or power. I hope you don’t think too poorly of me, but I took his offer.

JOHN BERNOULLI
And… then what happened?

MONEY GUY
I have worked for him, tirelessly, ever since. And he has paid me well, as promised. But it is soulless work. Day in and day out, I’ve toiled away under his command. Please John, I know it may seem silly, but cherish these opportunities to work on demos, to do fun projects, while you can. I wish I could go back to those days, but it’s too late for me. Don’t make the same mistake I did.

SSINROK
John, I’m done waiting for your answer. Are you with me, or are you against me?

JOHN BERNOULLI
I…

ASSASSIN
It’s not worth it John. Listen to what that weird guy said.

SCIENTIST
Think of what those pie tins did to my village! The horror! The horror!

SSINROK
This is your last chance.

JOHN BERNOULLI
I- I can’t do it Korniss, or Ssinrok, or whatever your name is. I can’t betray my friends like that. Overcoming these challenges, I’ve learned that there’s more to life than just knowing facts and equations.

SSINROK
I hate to do this, but you leave me no choice. Feel the wrath of my air cannon!

Ssinrok grabs the hidden air cannon, and fires. John, the Assassin, the Scientist, and Money Guy stand perplexed for several seconds, until they are abruptly toppled over by a large gust of wind.

JOHN BERNOULLI
Quick, follow me!

All four scramble to their feet and run off stage right. Ssinrok pursues.
