# Characters

**Protagonist**
- New member of build team

[Flaws]
- Doesn't like build team, looks down on all the projects
  - It's trivial
- Arrogant
- Too good at physics
- Doesn't trust others

[Arc]
- Will face hardships because of their flaws
- In the tech dump world, will slowly learn to overcome hubris
- Reaches critical points, realizes their errors
- Comes back with an appreciation for build team

**False Korniss**
- First person protagonist meets in the tech dump world
- At first seems nice, will later turn out to be evil (the "false" in "false korniss" tahaha)
- Actually the malevolent ruler of the tech dump world
- Secretly trying to test and recruit protagonist
- Very good at physics

[Flaws]
- Shares same flaws as protagonist
- Can't overcome flaws, will be defeated because of them
  
**Assassin**
- Protagonist's first companion in the tech dump world
- Suspicious of everyone at first, friendly and loyal after getting to know them
- The most physically capable of all the characters
- We think they die, but they come back to save the others

**Scientist**
- Decorated career, decides to set up a home lab in a small village
- Reluctant to let protagonist and assassin help with the pie tin babies
- Good physicist, second only to false korniss
- Butts heads with protagonist, eventually learns to work together

**Money Guy**
- Did physics undergrad, went into industry for the money, but dissatisfied with life
- Misses doing fun projects like in build team
- Convinces protagonist not to side with false korniss at the last minute
- Ends up quitting job and becoming physics teacher
